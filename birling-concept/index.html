Birling is a Canadian skateboard shop in downtown Ottawa. We offer a carefully curated selection of import and domestic skateboards, shoes, clothing and accessories throughout our meticulously handmade store. We also always have fresh, Little Victories Espresso on tap – some of the best coffee Ottawa has to offer! Everyone's welcome to cruise through and enjoy some sun at the store-front bar or on our street-side patio. Birling is the home we’ve created for our communal love of skateboarding to thrive in. Give us a call at (613) 695-4782 to chat.
